    Why Hop

    Customer Satisfaction - Profit

    The end goal of each venue is customer satisfaction and profit. Hop boosts both. There are two main points of revenue generation in a bar or club - the front door and the bartop. These two points are also the two biggest bottlenecks in every venue. Phase 1 of Hop fixes the front door bottleneck. Using the Hop Management app, bars and clubs are able to charge for entry all in one place in an efficient, simple, and seamless way while maintaining constant communication with bar-goers nearby should anything change. Communicate with your customers about line lengths and their correlated cover and line skip prices and change them with demand in real time. Attract new customers and reward regulars by pormoting specials, events, and more. Help customers get in the door faster and realize greater profits at the front door. We truly aim to digitize the nightlife experience for the benefit of everyone. Less waiting, more drinks - everyone's happy, venues and customers alike.

    Less Waiting, More Drinks

    Management App

    Hop Management App

    The app is divided into three parts. The first section is app management where venues can change prices for cover and skipping the line in real time. Maximize profit based on demand. The second section is the QR scanner where venues can scan each users QR code to charge them the entry fee. The third section is history where venues can see previous payments recieved and refund them should the need arise.

    How It Works

    Venues can collect entry fees from one screen

    • Choose what to charge

      Charge cover, Hop the Line (line skip), verify VIP, or check someone in for free. Report the line length with ease.
    • Scan the code

      Fast and simple. Once the payment is confirmed, scan the next QR.
