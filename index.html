    What’s Hop

    Nightlife In Your Pocket


    Hop is a mobile app that has everything you need for a night out. Before you leave for the night, check to see where your friends are and check your favorite venues to see line lengths, cover price, as well as how much it cost to hop the line. Once you pick a venue and get there, frontdoor security simply scans your QR code to charge you for cover, hopping the line, etc. Have a favorite venue? Become a VIP there by paying a monthly fee in exchange for what the venue offers i.e. unlimited hop the line’s. If you get in and decide to go somewhere else, check the app for line lengths and where your other friends are. If you’ve ever gone out and thought, “seems like there should be a more efficient way to do this,” there is, and our mission is to find it, perfect it, and provide it to you!

    Less Waiting, More Drinks

    How Does It Work?

    Secure - Fast - Simple

    1. 1
      Secure

      When the app opens, FaceID verification allows access to the QR code. Each time the app is opened, a new code is generated.

    2. 2
      Fast

      Once the QR code is opened, all the venue does is scan it to charge you for cover, etc.

    3. 3
      Simple

      No need to buy anything in advance. No need for cash or ATM’s.

    Friends

    Users Can Only Be Seen When At A Venue


    When a user walks into a venue, they automatically appear on the map to their friends. When a user leaves the venue, they automatically disappear from the map. Users are only visible on the map to their friends when at a venue. There is no 24/7 tracking and no need to post or update where you are going. Social privacy at its best.
